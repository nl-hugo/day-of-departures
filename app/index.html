Carriers

The largest carrier in the Netherlands is NS by far. It's national branch covers most of the cities in the country, while NS International covers major stations alongs the routes to Germany and Belgium onwards.

Of the smaller carriers, Arriva is the largest, covering large parts in the North an East of the country. Other carriers operate regional services at minor stations outside the 'Randstad'.

Stations close to the border are serviced by carriers in cross-border lines.
